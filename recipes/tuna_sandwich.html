Tuna Sandwich Recipe

Description

Tuna sandwiches are great for a quick and easy meal. Best eaten with your hands.

Ingredients:

  • Can of tuna
  • Mayo
  • Bread
  • Olives, tomatoes or any other complement you like

Steps:

  1. In a bowl, use your fork to mix the tuna with mayo and some sal and pepper
  2. Cut two slices of bread
  3. Spread the tuna mix on top of the bread slices
  4. Add any other complement you wish
