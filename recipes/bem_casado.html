Bem Casado Recipe

Description

Bem casado is a typical Brazilian dish, usually eaten for lunch. Its a simple dish composed of rice, beans, steak and french fries. Extremely delicious.

Ingredients:

  • Rice
  • Beans
  • Steak
  • Potatoes

Steps:

  1. Cook the rice with boiling water
  2. Cook the beans
  3. Season the steak with salt and pepper and grill it with some butter
  4. Slice thin strips of potatoes
  5. Fry the potatoes
  6. Serve all ingredients in the same plate
