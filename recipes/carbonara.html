Carbonara Recipe

Description

The carbonara is a fairly simple dish which uses few ingredients, but when done right is an explosion of flavors

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti
  • Pancetta
  • Pecorino Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Black Pepper
  • Parsley
  • Garlic

Steps:

  1. Boil water
  2. Add spaghetti to boiling water
  3. Add two tablespoons of olive oil to a hot pan
  4. Add pancetta to the pan
  5. Chop thin slices of the garlic
  6. Add the garlic to the pancetta
  7. Pancetta is done cooking when close to golden
  8. Save a few scoops of the pasta waster in a bowl
  9. Crack two eggs in a bowl and mix it up with a lot of black pepper, parsley, and pecorino cheese
  10. Drain the pasta once done cooking
  11. In a bowl add the pancetta and pasta and mix them together
  12. Add to the pasta the egg, pepper and pecorino mixture (don't let the eggs cook too much)
